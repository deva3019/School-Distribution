Frequently Asked Questions

What do I do if I forget my password?

Contact your Regional Distributor. They have a "1-Click Reset" button on their dashboard that will instantly reset your password to the default format, allowing you to log back in immediately.

Do I need to create an account?

No. All 200+ school accounts have been pre-provisioned by the Government using your official School ID to ensure maximum security and ease of access.

Can I request more items than the stock available?

Yes, you submit your actual requirement. If the stock is lower than your requirement, the Distributor has the authority to partially fulfill your request based on current availability.
